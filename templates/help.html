Who are we?

Plant Pulse is an innovative platform dedicated to helping people monitor and manage their plants efficiently. By integrating sensor technology with a web-based interface, we empower plant enthusiasts to track environmental factors and ensure optimal conditions for plant growth.

What's the purpose of the app?

The purpose of the Plant Pulse app is to provide real-time data and insights into your plants' health. Our goal is to create a user-friendly interface that allows gardeners, hobbyists, and professionals alike to monitor plant vitals such as soil moisture, pH levels, and environmental conditions.

How to use the app?

Using Plant Pulse is simple! Log into your account, connect your plant sensors, and start monitoring your plants' health through our intuitive dashboard. You can also view historical data and receive actionable tips to ensure the best growth environment.
